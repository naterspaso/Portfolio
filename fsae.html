Bronco Motorsports FSAE

Bronco Motorsports is a collegiate engineering team which focuses on designing, building, and racing a student-built, formula-style racecar. Every year, our team competes at Michigan International Speedway against around 120 other universities from around the world. Last season, we finished 39th overall in the competition. However, we had made many small mistakes which cost us lots of points. So this year, we are coming back with a refined car and team to finish unfinished business.

I am the captain of the Hubs and Uprights subsystem, which is part of the Vehicle Dynamics system of the car. I am responsible for the design, manufacturing, and testing of the hubs and uprights of the car.

Accomplishments

  • Made a design that aligned with the overall vehicle goals for the year: reliability & simplicity
  • Collaborated with brakes and steering subsystems to make compatible and complementing designs
  • Scored 25/30 for the "structures" portion of vehicle design at the competition in Michigan, one of the highest scores for that category.
